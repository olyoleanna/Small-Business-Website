Slow-made, wearable and functional art by Kars. The Clay Project PH is a one-woman team brand producing limited-batch and slow-made artisanal jewelries and whatnot.

Founded in 2020 in the midst of the pandemic, Kars found a way on how to express herself and share her heart through art. Using Polymer Clay as her primary medium, she was able to thoughtfully and lovingly design, create and assemble one of a kind statement pieces. The goal of The Clay Project is for their customers to make them feel confident and empowered while helping mother earth through their sustainable pieces, one wearable art at a time.
